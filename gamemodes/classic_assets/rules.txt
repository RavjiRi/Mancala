\1bold\1OBJECTIVE:\2

Both players try to collect the most stones in the mancala store as possible

The game ends when at least one side of the board is empty
The winner is the player with the most stones

\1bold\1PARTS OF THE BOARD:\2

The mancala has 6 pits on each side of the board
The large pit to the right of the player is their store
The large pit to the left of the player is the opponents store

\1bold\1HOW TO PLAY:\2

When a pit is clicked, the stones will be dropped one by one clockwise around the board
The stones will not be dropped in the opponents store
If the last stone lands in the players own store, they get to go again
You can only click on your side of the board
